Main Commands

The hunt, praycurse and huntbot commands are enabled by default

hunt ~ Toggles the reminder for owo hunt

battle ~ Toggles the reminder for owo battle

praycurse ~ Toggles the reminder for owo pray & owo curse

huntbot ~ Toggles the reminder for owo huntbot

show ~ Shows what is enabled/disabled
